Smart Contact Manager

Smart Contact Manager is your personal digital assistant for securely managing your contacts. Whether you're a student, professional, or business owner, this app gives you a smart and intuitive platform to store, update, and organize your contacts easily.

✨ Features:

  • 🔐 User Registration & Login with role-based authentication
  • 👤 Personalized dashboard for each user
  • 📇 Add, View, Update & Delete contacts
  • 🔍 Search contacts by name or phone number
  • 📂 Upload and display contact images
  • ⚙️ Update your user profile and settings
  • 🚪 Secure logout functionality

This project is designed for full-stack development learners and can be enhanced with advanced features like pagination, REST APIs, or a React frontend. It's not just a project — it's a demonstration of security, usability, and real-world development best practices.
